<p><p>The Semiconductor Backend Visual Inspection System market comprises primarily two types: Optical Based and Infrared Types. Optical Based systems utilize visible light to detect defects on semiconductor wafers, leveraging high-resolution cameras for detailed imaging. In contrast, Infrared Type systems employ infrared radiation to identify thermal signatures and defects that may not be visible to the naked eye, offering advantages in assessing material integrity and identifying issues like overheating. Both types enhance quality control in semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>

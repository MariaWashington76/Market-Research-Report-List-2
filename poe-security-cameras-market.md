<p><p>The PoE (Power over Ethernet) security camera market includes three main types: bullet, dome, and PTZ (pan-tilt-zoom). Bullet cameras are compact and designed for long-range viewing, making them suitable for outdoor monitoring. Dome cameras have a sleek design, offering discreet surveillance with a wider field of view, often used in indoor environments. PTZ cameras provide flexibility with remote control capabilities, allowing users to pan, tilt, and zoom for detailed monitoring over broader areas, combining versatility with functionality.</p></p>

<p><p>The residential mist cooling system market comprises three main types based on pressure: low, medium, and high-pressure systems. Low-pressure mist cooling systems use gravity to distribute water, making them cost-effective but less efficient in high temperatures. Medium-pressure systems provide improved cooling effects and increased durability, suitable for moderate climates. High-pressure mist cooling systems operate under significant pressure for fine mist creation, delivering superior cooling performance and energy efficiency, making them ideal for hot and arid environments.</p></p>

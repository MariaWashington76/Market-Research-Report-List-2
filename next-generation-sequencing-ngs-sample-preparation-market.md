<p><p>The Next Generation Sequencing (NGS) sample preparation market encompasses several key technologies. Sequencing by Synthesis (SBS) utilizes fluorescently labeled nucleotides for base incorporation detection. Ion Semiconductor Sequencing measures pH changes during nucleotide incorporation, offering rapid processing. Single Molecule Real Time (SMRT) Sequencing captures real-time DNA synthesis by identifying fluorescently tagged nucleotides. Nanopore Sequencing involves threading DNA through nanopores to analyze sequence data in real-time. Each method presents unique advantages and applications in genomics and molecular biology.</p></p>

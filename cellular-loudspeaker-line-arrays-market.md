<p><p>Cellular loudspeaker line arrays are classified into two main types: active and passive. Active line arrays integrate built-in amplification and signal processing, allowing for enhanced control over sound quality and dynamics, making them ideal for large events and venues. In contrast, passive line arrays require external amplifiers and processors, providing flexibility in system design but demanding more setup effort. Both types serve distinct market needs, with active arrays favored for convenience and efficiency, while passive arrays appeal to custom installations and traditional setups.</p></p>

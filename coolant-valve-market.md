<p><p>The coolant valve market comprises various types, including 2-way valves, 3-way valves, and others. 2-way valves control the flow of coolant in a single direction, ideal for simple on-off applications. Conversely, 3-way valves manage two flow paths, enabling mixing or diverting coolant, making them suitable for more complex systems. Other types may include motorized or thermostatic valves, catering to specific needs in industrial, automotive, and HVAC applications, ensuring optimal temperature regulation and system efficiency across diverse sectors.</p></p>

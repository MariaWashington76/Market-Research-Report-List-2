<p><p>In-Mold Labeling (IML) is a process where labels are integrated into the mold during the production of plastic products, enhancing aesthetics and functionality. The market comprises three primary types: </p><p>1. **Injection Molding IML**: Labels are inserted into molds before molten plastic is injected, ideal for complex products.</p><p>   </p><p>2. **Blow Molding IML**: Labels are applied during the formation of hollow plastic containers, commonly used in bottles.</p><p>3. **Thermoforming IML**: Labels are incorporated while heating and forming sheet plastic, suitable for food packaging and trays.</p></p>

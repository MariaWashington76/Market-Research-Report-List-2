<p><p>Grain Oriented Electrical Steel Sheet (GOES) is categorized primarily into High-Grade Oriented (HGO) and Conventional Grain Oriented (CGO) types. HGO sheets feature an enhanced magnetic property, optimizing energy efficiency and minimizing losses in transformers and electric motors, making them ideal for high-performance applications. Conversely, CGO sheets offer sufficient magnetic performance but at a lower cost, suitable for standard applications. Both types play crucial roles in the electrical steel market, supporting advancements in energy-efficient technologies and various industrial uses.</p></p>

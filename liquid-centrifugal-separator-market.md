<p><p>The liquid centrifugal separator market comprises two primary types: filtration centrifuges and sedimentation centrifuges. Filtration centrifuges utilize a filter medium to separate solids from liquids, effectively removing impurities and enhancing product quality. Sedimentation centrifuges, on the other hand, leverage centrifugal force to separate materials based on density differences, allowing heavier particles to settle at the bottom. Both types are essential in various industries, including food and beverage, pharmaceuticals, and wastewater treatment, promoting efficiency and product purity.</p></p>

<p><p>The organic apple juice market is primarily divided into two types: 100% pure juice and juice concentrate. 100% pure juice is made by directly pressing organic apples, retaining their natural flavor and nutrients without any additives. Juice concentrate, on the other hand, involves removing water from the juice to create a more concentrated form, which is then reconstituted with water before packaging. This method often lowers production costs and extends shelf life, catering to different consumer preferences within the organic segment.</p></p>

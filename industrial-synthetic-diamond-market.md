<p><ul><li>RVD Diamond Grain</li><li>MBD Diamond Grain</li><li>SCD Diamond Grain</li><li>SMD Diamond Grain</li><li>DMD Diamond Grain</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial synthetic diamond market comprises several types differentiated by their production processes and applications. RVD (Random Vapor Deposition) diamond grains are known for their uniformity and are used in cutting tools. MBD (Microcrystalline Diamond) grains are valued for their hardness and are often used in abrasive applications. SCD (Single Crystal Diamond) grains offer superior clarity and performance in high-precision tasks. SMD (Sub-Microcrystalline Diamond) grains combine features of both micro and single crystals, while DMD (Diamond Microcrystalline Diamond) grains are noted for their fine particle size, enhancing various industrial applications.</p></p>

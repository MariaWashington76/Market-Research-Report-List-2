<p><strong>The Custom Hearing Aids Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>In-The-Canal Hearing Aid</li><li>In-The-Ear Hearing Aid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The custom hearing aids market includes two primary types: In-The-Canal (ITC) and In-The-Ear (ITE) hearing aids. ITC devices are tailored to fit partially inside the ear canal, offering discreteness and moderate amplification, making them suitable for mild to moderate hearing loss. Conversely, ITE hearing aids are larger, filling the outer ear, providing greater battery life and advanced features, catering to a wider range of hearing loss severity. Both types emphasize personalization for enhanced comfort and performance.</p></p>

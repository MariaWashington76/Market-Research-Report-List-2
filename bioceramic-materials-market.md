Composite bioceramics combine organic and inorganic components for improved performance. Alumina bioceramics are known for their mechanical strength and wear resistance, commonly used in implants. Other bioceramic materials include bioactive glasses and zirconia, utilized for specific biomedical applications.</p></p>

<p><p>The automatic car washer market features two prominent types: Gantry car washes and conveyor tunnel systems. Gantry car washes utilize a fixed frame that moves over the vehicle, applying cleaning solutions and brushes while the car remains stationary. In contrast, conveyor tunnel systems involve the vehicle driving onto a conveyor belt that transports it through a series of washing stations. Both methods offer efficient, time-saving solutions for vehicle cleaning, catering to varying consumer preferences regarding speed and thoroughness.</p></p>

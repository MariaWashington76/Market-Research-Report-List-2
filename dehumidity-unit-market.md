<p><p>The dehumidity unit market encompasses various types designed for specific moisture control needs. Cooling dehumidifiers utilize refrigeration to condense moisture from the air, ideal for residential and commercial spaces. Rotary dehumidifiers employ a rotating desiccant wheel to absorb humidity, suitable for industrial applications. Pipeline dehumidifiers are integrated into HVAC systems to maintain optimal humidity levels in large facilities. Electroosmosis dehumidifiers use electrical forces to draw moisture from materials, often used in historic building preservation and specialized drying processes.</p></p>

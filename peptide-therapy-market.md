<p><p>Peptide therapy market is categorized by routes of administration, each offering unique advantages. The parenteral route, often involving injections, ensures rapid absorption and effectiveness for peptides that degrade in the digestive tract. The mucosal route, which includes nasal or buccal administration, facilitates quick systemic effects while avoiding first-pass metabolism. The oral route aims for patient convenience but faces challenges with peptide stability and absorption. The transdermal route allows for steady delivery via the skin, enhancing patient compliance and minimizing side effects.</p></p>

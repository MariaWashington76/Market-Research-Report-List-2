<p><strong>The Cold Rolled Electrical Steel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Oriented Electrical Steel</li><li>Non-oriented Electrical Steel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cold-rolled electrical steel market is primarily divided into two types: oriented electrical steel (OE) and non-oriented electrical steel (NOE). Oriented electrical steel is engineered to exhibit high magnetic permeability in specific directions, making it ideal for use in transformers and high-performance motors. In contrast, non-oriented electrical steel maintains uniform magnetic properties in all directions, which is suitable for applications like electric vehicle motors and general electrical machinery. Both types are crucial for enhancing energy efficiency in electrical devices.</p></p>

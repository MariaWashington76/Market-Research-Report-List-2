<p><strong>The Handheld Fiber Fusion Splicer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cladding Alignment</li><li>Core Alignment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The handheld fiber fusion splicer market is divided into two main types: cladding alignment and core alignment. Cladding alignment splicers use the outer cladding of the fiber to ensure proper alignment, making them simpler and often more affordable for basic applications. In contrast, core alignment splicers precisely align the fiber cores for higher precision, resulting in lower splice loss and better performance, making them ideal for demanding environments. Each type serves different user needs based on application requirements and budget constraints.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>Passive Elastomeric Brackets</li><li>Active Engine Bracket</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive engine bracket market consists of two primary types: passive elastomeric brackets and active engine brackets. Passive elastomeric brackets utilize flexible rubber or polymer materials to absorb vibrations and support engine stability without the need for external energy. In contrast, active engine brackets employ advanced technologies, such as actuators, to dynamically adjust motor mount stiffness in response to vibrations and driving conditions, enhancing performance and comfort. Both types play crucial roles in improving vehicle performance and noise reduction.</p></p>

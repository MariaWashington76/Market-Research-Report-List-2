<p><p>The Natural Gas Separation Membrane Module Market is primarily categorized into two types: roll and plate modules. Roll modules consist of permeable membranes wound in a cylindrical form, offering compact designs and high surface area for efficient gas separation. Plate modules, on the other hand, feature flat membranes arranged in a stacked configuration, providing ease of maintenance and scalability. Both designs are utilized to enhance the separation of gases in natural gas processing, contributing to more efficient and environmentally friendly operations.</p></p>

<p><p>The PET Stretch Blow Molding Machines market is segmented into two main types: Single Step and Two Step machines. Single Step PET Stretch Blow Molding Machines combine the processes of preform production and bottle blowing into one continuous operation, offering efficiency and lower labor costs. In contrast, Two Step PET Stretch Blow Molding Machines involve separate processes for creating preforms and performing the blow molding, allowing for greater flexibility and scalability in production. Both types cater to diverse manufacturing needs in the beverage and packaging industries.</p></p>

<p><p>Disk lasers are categorized into three market types based on their power output: low power, mid power, and high power. Low power disk lasers, typically under 500 watts, are used for applications such as medical and engraving. Mid power lasers, ranging from 500 watts to a few kilowatts, serve in manufacturing tasks like cutting and welding. High power disk lasers, exceeding several kilowatts, are employed in heavy industrial applications, including metal fabrication and high-precision material processing.</p></p>

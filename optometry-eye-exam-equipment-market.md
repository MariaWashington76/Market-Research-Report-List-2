<p><p>The optometry and eye exam equipment market includes various specialized tools essential for eye care. Optical coherence tomography (OCT) provides detailed imaging of the retina, while fundus cameras capture high-resolution photos of the eye's interior. Perimeters assess visual fields, and topography maps the corneal curvature. Lensmeters measure lens prescriptions, and chart projectors display visual tests for patients. Wavefront analyzers evaluate aberrations in vision, enhancing the accuracy of corrective lenses. Together, these instruments are vital for comprehensive eye examinations.</p></p>

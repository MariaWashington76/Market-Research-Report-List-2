<p><ul><li>Direct Type</li><li>Indirect Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mass flow controllers (MFCs) are essential devices in various industries for regulating gas and liquid flow rates. The market is primarily segmented into two types: direct and indirect. Direct type MFCs measure mass flow through thermal principles, effectively managing flow in real-time with high precision. Indirect type MFCs, on the other hand, derive flow measurements through differential pressure or volumetric methods, offering benefits in certain applications like high-pressure systems. Both types cater to specific needs across diverse sectors, including semiconductor manufacturing and chemical processing.</p></p>

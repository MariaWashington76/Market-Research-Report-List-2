<p><p>Monochrome graphic displays are categorized mainly into three types: green screen, white screen, and amber screen displays. Green screens utilize green phosphors for high contrast and are often preferred in low-light environments due to eye strain reduction. White screens offer bright, clear visuals suitable for various applications, especially in well-lit conditions. Amber screens, on the other hand, provide a balance between visibility and eye comfort, making them ideal for specialized uses such as industrial equipment. Each type serves distinct needs based on visibility and user preference.</p></p>
